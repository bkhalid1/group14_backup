What We Do

We are a group of 3rd year Heriot-Watt Computer Science/Systems Students who are making a Social Network app 'MashBook' that is aimed to be used by people with a creative background. .

Market

Research

Some of our group took time and looked at other social networks and created a report to show us what they had in common and how our own Social Network would differ form the rest or improve existing features available on them.

Public

Feedback

We plan to ask people outside of the group regularly throughout the development of MashBook so that we can always have an idea of how the general audience will find our app and use that feedback to make improvements.

Security

We want MashBook to have the very best security it can so that our users can feel safe to use personal information to create accounts and feel comfortable knowing that it wont be used/ stolen by others.

Social

Network

We hope that MashBook can be a clean Social Network and be an environment for artists to share, comment and gain ideas so that they can improve projects they are working on.
